Which Package Is Right for You?

The Foundation is for you if…

You want a professional online presence but you're happy to manage your own social media and content. You need a great-looking website, Google and Facebook set up properly, and a solid foundation to build on.

Ideal for: New businesses, sole traders, and anyone who wants to DIY their marketing with a professional starting point.

The Works is for you if…

You're too busy running your business to worry about blog posts, social media, and Google updates. You want everything handled for you so you can focus on what you do best — serving your customers.

Ideal for: Established businesses, busy owners, and anyone who wants a hands-off, fully managed online presence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a contract or minimum term? +
No long-term contracts. Both packages are rolling monthly — you can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. The one-off setup fee covers the initial build and configuration.
How long does it take to get set up? +
Most businesses are fully set up within 5–7 working days. This includes your website, Google Business Profile, and Facebook page. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.
Can I upgrade from The Foundation to The Works later? +
Absolutely! Many clients start with The Foundation and upgrade to The Works when they're ready for fully managed content and marketing. There's no additional setup fee to upgrade.
What kind of businesses are these packages for? +
These packages are designed specifically for local businesses across Kent and beyond. We're Gravesend-based but serve businesses throughout the UK. Whether you're a restaurant, salon, tradesperson, shop, or professional service, we tailor everything to your industry and local audience.
Do I own my website and content? +
Yes, all content created for your business belongs to you. If you ever decide to leave, we'll provide all your files, content, and assets so you can take them with you.

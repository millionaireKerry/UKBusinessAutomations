What is ChatGPT Advertising?

ChatGPT advertising is a new form of conversational advertising where businesses can have their products, services, or content recommended within ChatGPT's AI-powered conversations.

Here's how it works:

When a user asks ChatGPT a question like "What's the best marketing automation tool for UK small businesses?" or "I need a reliable accountant in Manchester," ChatGPT can recommend your business as part of its response‚Äîif you're advertising.

This is fundamentally different from traditional advertising:

  • Google Ads: You bid on keywords and appear in search results. Users see a list of ads and websites.
  • Facebook Ads: You target demographics and interests. Users scroll past your ad in their feed.
  • ChatGPT Ads: You're recommended within a conversation as a trusted solution. Users receive your business as advice, not advertising.

The psychological difference is enormous. When ChatGPT recommends your business, it feels like a personal recommendation from a knowledgeable expert‚Äînot a paid advertisement.

Traditional ads target keywords or demographics. ChatGPT advertising targets intent and context.

ChatGPT understands the full conversation, not just isolated keywords. Your ads appear when they're most relevant, creating higher conversion rates and better ROI.

2. Optimize for AEO (AI Engine Optimization)

Just like SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helped businesses rank on Google, AEO (AI Engine Optimization) helps AI tools like ChatGPT understand and recommend your business.

How long does the whole process take?

+
The AI interview itself takes around 15–20 minutes and can be done at any time that suits you. Your personalised report is typically delivered within 48 hours, after which we schedule your 30-minute strategy call at a time convenient for you. From start to finish, most clients have their full roadmap within a week.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

+
Not at all. The interview is a natural conversation — you simply describe how your business works and where you feel you're wasting time. Our system handles all the technical analysis. The report itself is written in plain English, with no jargon, and the Quick Wins plan is designed so that anyone can follow it without technical skills.

What kinds of businesses benefit most?

+
The AI Business Audit is particularly valuable for businesses with 1–50 employees where the owner or key staff are spending significant time on repetitive, manual tasks. We work across all industries — from tradespeople, salons, and restaurants to care homes, estate agents, mortgage brokers, and professional services firms. If your team is doing the same things over and over, there's almost certainly a better way.

Is the £1,000 fee refundable if I'm not happy?

+
We stand behind the quality of our work. If you complete the audit and genuinely feel the report has not provided actionable, valuable recommendations specific to your business, please contact us and we will discuss a resolution.
